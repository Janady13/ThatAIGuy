Key Moments in AI History

1950 – Turing Test

Alan Turing proposes the Imitation Game.

1956 – Dartmouth Workshop

The term “AI” is coined.

2012 – Deep Learning Breakthrough

AlexNet wins ImageNet, revitalizing neural nets.

2016 – AlphaGo vs. Lee Sedol

Milestone for reinforcement learning.

2020 – GPT-3

Unprecedented text generation scale.

2022 – ChatGPT

LLMs reach mainstream via chat interfaces.
