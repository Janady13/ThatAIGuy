The truth about AI

What AI does well

  • Pattern completion, summarization, remixing known styles
  • Fast drafting, code scaffolding, basic analysis
  • Search + synthesis for common knowledge

What AI does poorly

  • Ground truth without sources (hallucinations)
  • Up-to-the-minute facts without browsing
  • Nuanced judgment without human review

Safety & ethics

Respect creators, cite sources, protect privacy, and design with consent. AI should expand human potential—never replace dignity.

How to verify outputs

Check dates, compare multiple sources, and test code. When in doubt, ask for citations or rerun with web search enabled.
